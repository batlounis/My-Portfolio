iPad Magazine

Visual Design, 2011

The iPad Magazine is a group design project, part of the Interaction Design Fundamentals course. My role was conducting visual design and presentation development.

The pre-design consisted of personas, mood boards, and a competitive analysis.

Next, we built a design language using typeface, color palette, and oblique shapes distinctive to our magazine. During our critique we received positive reviews from fellow students and faculty.
